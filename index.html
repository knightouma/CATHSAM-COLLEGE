Diploma In Information Technology

The programme intends to equip students with a wide range of software, technical and technological ICT skills required in the industry at diploma level. On graduation, the student is expected to develop and apply IT solutions to solve practical business problems.

  1. 2 Year Course
  2. 5 semesters
  3. Full time

Diploma In Business Information Technology

This programme is designed to produce information and business savvy graduates by providing a sound background in advanced problem solving, information analysis and project management.

  1. 2 Year Course
  2. 5 semesters
  3. Full time

Diploma in Procurement and Contract management

This programme will offer skills necessary to build successful careers in procurement industry by providing a conceptual foundation in Supply Chain management, logistics and procurement.

  1. 2 Year Course
  2. 5 semesters
  3. Full time

Certificate in Information Technology

This programme offers skills and training in a wide range of software, technical and technological ICT skills required for the industry. Graduates will be able to develop IT solutions to address practical business problems using an engineering and technological approach.

  1. 6 months
  2. 2 semesters
  3. Full time

Certificate in Procurement and Contract Management

This programme will offer skills on how to effectively and efficiently manage the activities of the supply chain. Emphasis will be on the importance of the supply chain and the role it plays in an organization’s strategic development.

  1. 6 months
  2. 2 semesters
  3. Full time

Certificate in Development Studies
