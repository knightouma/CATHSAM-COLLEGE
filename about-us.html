HomeAbout Us

Cathsam traces its roots back to 2015 when it was founded by Eng.B William as a Centre providing ICT training. Driven by a passion to meet the high demand for quality education, the institution embarked on a 5 year strategic blueprint manual that outlined the aspiration to attain University status. Thereafter, the institution entered a decisive phase towards achieving University status when it broke ground and constructed a multi-million University Complex along Mombasa Road, launched by His Excellency Uhuru Kenyatta, President of the Republic of Kenya. After successful inspection by the Commission of University Education, the institution was awarded a Letter of Authority to offer university programmes, consequently giving rise to Cathsam University in August 2020 Since inception, over 10,000 students have graduated as the Varsityâ€™s drive to enrich lives through education grows every day; this has been achieved through significant growth of programmes offered, development of modern infrastructure and a team of distinguished faculty who are passionate trainers, to meet the demand for quality higher education in Kenya and beyond. The University, which has a current population of 2000 students, is committed to scaling the heights of education in the world with a mission to provide holistic higher education through promoting excellence in research, integrating innovation and technology in learning and developing well rounded citizens to impact the social, political and economic world..
